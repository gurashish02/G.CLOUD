About

Portrait pending

I'm Gurashish Kaur, a photo editor and digital artist working primarily in Photoshop. My work sits between repair and invention — sometimes it's a portrait that needs its light rebuilt, sometimes it's a scene that never existed until I put its pieces together.

I care about images holding up under a close look: skin that still looks like skin, shadows that agree with each other, seams that don't announce themselves.
